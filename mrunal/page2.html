Introduction to Resume Building

Everything You Need to Know to Create a Winning Resume

What is a Resume?

A resume is a document that showcases your skills, experience, and education. It helps employers understand your qualifications for a job or internship.

How to Make a Resume?

  1. Choose a Format: Chronological, Functional, or Combination.
  2. Add Contact Information: Name, email, phone number, and LinkedIn (if available).
  3. Write a Strong Summary: A brief introduction about yourself and your career goals.
  4. List Work Experience: Include job roles, companies, and responsibilities.
  5. Education Section: Mention degrees, universities, and graduation years.
  6. Skills & Certifications: Highlight relevant skills and any certifications.
  7. Additional Sections: Projects, achievements, or extracurricular activities.
  8. Proofread & Format: Ensure proper formatting, grammar, and readability.
